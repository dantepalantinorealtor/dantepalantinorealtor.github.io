Our Story

Trusted Service.
Exceptional Results.

Palantino Real Estate is a boutique luxury residential practice serving Philadelphia and the Jersey Shore. We combine deep local knowledge with a modern, client-first approach — delivering the premium presentation and personalized attention that discerning buyers and sellers expect.

From historic Society Hill townhomes to oceanfront estates in Avalon, we curate every transaction with the same editorial care we bring to every detail. Our market is anything but generic. Neither is our service.

We specialize in helping clients navigate two of the East Coast's most compelling real estate markets — the storied neighborhoods of Philadelphia and the coveted shoreline communities of the Jersey coast.
